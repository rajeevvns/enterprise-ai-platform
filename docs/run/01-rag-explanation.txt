RAG stands for Retrieval-Augmented Generation. It is a framework that combines traditional retrieval-based methods with generative models to enhance the performance of natural language processing tasks. In RAG, a model first retrieves relevant documents or information from a large dataset and then uses that information to generate more accurate and contextually relevant responses. This approach leverages the strengths of both retrieval and generation, improving the quality of the output by grounding it in real-world data. RAG is particularly useful in tasks like question answering and conversational AI.
